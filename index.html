What is ClickKit?

ClickKit is a toolkit of advanced add-ons for ClickUp. It includes automated Backup & Archive Management, Smart Forms Builder, Custom Dashboards, and Automation Studio—designed for agencies and growing teams.

How does the backup feature work?

ClickKit automatically backs up your ClickUp data on a schedule you control. You can restore entire workspaces, lists, or individual tasks if anything goes wrong. Your backups are securely stored and easily accessible.

How does the Archive Manager work?

We provide a better interface to browse your archived tasks and lists, with bulk restore capabilities and analytics on your completed work. Never lose track of archived data again.

Who is it for?

ClickKit is built for ClickUp agencies and power teams that need more than the native features offer—specifically around backup, forms, reporting, and complex automation.

How does it integrate with ClickUp?

ClickKit uses the official ClickUp API and webhooks to integrate seamlessly. Your data stays in ClickUp, but we give you new ways to protect, view, capture, and automate it.

What about the reporting dashboards?

Our dashboard builder lets you create client-ready reports with your own branding. Visualize velocity, burndown, and other custom metrics that native ClickUp dashboards miss.

Can I migrate my existing forms?

Yes! We can import your existing ClickUp form configurations so you don't have to start from scratch. Then you can add logic and file uploads on top.

Does this replace ClickUp?

No, ClickKit extends ClickUp. You continue to use ClickUp for task management, while ClickKit handles backup protection, advanced forms, external reporting, and complex automations.
